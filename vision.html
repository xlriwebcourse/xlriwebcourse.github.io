Vision

Ignorantia Juris non excusat or ignorance of law is not an excuse. The law in India does not excuse legal infractions resulting in long legal battles, compromising the goodwill of the institution. To prevent said situation, we at Juris Expo provide a complete legal solution, including expert advice to cope with the demands of NTPC labour compliences and other legal matters pertaining to labour laws. Juris Expo strives towards imparting premium legal education and consultancy for NTPC in order to avoid consequences of non-compliance. Our experts have vast industry experience which ensures innovative legal solutions and advice.
